Join Us at ConnectedLibFEST

  • Do you work in a small or rural library?
  • Do you work with small and rural library staff?
  • Have you thought about how connected learning and small and rural libraries are a match made in heaven?
  • Do you want to learn more about connected learning and small and rural libraries and communities?

Did you answer “yes” to any of these questions?

If so, then CLFest is the event for you.

CLFest is the first ever event specifically focused on connected learning in small and rural libraries and communities. This virtual experience takes place on Wednesday and Thursday, March 15 and 16 - from 1-5PM Eastern, noon to 4PM Central, 11AM to 3PM Mountain, 10AM to 2PM Pacific. Each day, brings together library staff, researchers, and community members to learn from and with each other.

Topics:

  • What is connected learning and why is it something small and rural libraries should know about?
  • How do I get started designing connected learning services in my small or rural community?
  • What are some ways to build community-centered connected learning services?
  • How to transform existing services, using your library’s and community’s assets, to align with connected learning?
And others that you might suggest. Call for proposals and registration coming soon!
